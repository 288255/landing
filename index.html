Quick Start

Use our raster tiles instantly:

https://288255.xyz/tiles/{z}/{x}/{y}.png

Replace {z}, {x}, {y} with your desired zoom and coordinates.

Example (Leaflet.js):

L.tileLayer('https://288255.xyz/tiles/{z}/{x}/{y}.png', {
  attribution: '© OpenStreetMap contributors',
  maxZoom: 19
}).addTo(map);

About OpenFreeMap

OpenFreeMap generously provides the global vector tile infrastructure that powers this service.

How it works: We fetch vector tiles from OpenFreeMap and render them into raster tiles on demand. This lets you use a simple raster tile endpoint, while benefiting from the flexibility and freshness of vector data.

We are grateful to OpenFreeMap for making open, high-quality map data available to everyone.

Learn more or support them at openfreemap.org.
