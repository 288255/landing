Quick Start

Use our raster tiles instantly:

https://288255.xyz/tiles/{z}/{x}/{y}.png

Replace {z}, {x}, {y} with your desired zoom and coordinates.

Example (Leaflet.js):

L.tileLayer('https://288255.xyz/tiles/{z}/{x}/{y}.png', {
  attribution: '© OpenStreetMap contributors',
  maxZoom: 19
}).addTo(map);

How 288255.xyz works.

288255.xyz is built on top of OpenFreemap's vector tile service.
288255.xyz takes the vector tiles from OpenFreeMap and renders them into raster tiles on-the-fly, providing a simple raster tile endpoint for easy integration into mapping applications.
Learn more at openfreemap.org.

Caching

  • Images cached for 1 month
  • Inactive tiles removed after 2 weeks
